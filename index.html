Came to Tea

DATES: Until September 2019

Age guide: 0—3

Duration: 40 mins

An immersive exhibition of the world of Judith Kerr’s creatures. Join The Tiger for tea and hear the tale of his surprise visit to Sophie and her mummy. Open the door and take a trip into the real-life world of Mog the Forgetful Cat.

We strongly advise booking in advance to avoid disappointment!
